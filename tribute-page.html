Post Malone

It was never my plan to fall in love with this man

During the pandemic I decided to listen to This Is Post Malone on Spotify while doing crochet and ended up enjoying what I heard.

Here's a time line of Mr. Post's life:

  • 1995 - Austin Richard Post is born in Syracuse, New York. His sign is Cancer.
  • 2004 - Austin moves to Grapevine, Texas with his family.
  • 2012 - Leon DeChino becomes a popular sensation (he is very famous in Brazil).
  • 2015 - Post Malone releases his hit, White Iverson.
  • 2016 - Releases his mixtape, August 26th, on the 12th of May. And then his debut album, Stoney, in December.
  • 2018 - The album beerbongs & Bentleys comes out and he peaks at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 with the single Rockstar, featuring 21 Savage. Malone breaks Michael Jackson's record for most weeks on Billboard's Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.
  • 2019 - Posty releases his third album, Hollywood's Bleeding, recorded at his home in Utah.
  • 2022 - After three years, the fourth album comes out: Twelve Carat Toothache. Posty performs at Rock in Rio, where I see him live for the first time.
  • 2023 - Posty opens up a Raisin Canes store (because why the hell not?) that I sure would love to visit someday. His fifth album titled Austin comes out in July.
